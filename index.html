About Becky

Becky Plank is a dedicated Special Education Teacher with nearly a decade of experience at Desoto County Schools in Olive Branch, Mississippi. She began her journey in education as a Teaching Assistant before earning her position as a full-time Special Education Teacher in 2017, a role she continues to hold with deep commitment and passion.

Prior to entering K–12 education, Becky worked as a Pre-School Teacher at Maples Learning Center, where she planned and directed play-based, developmentally appropriate curriculum for young children. That early childhood foundation gave her a lasting understanding of how children grow, learn, and thrive at every stage.

Becky bridges the gap between potential and performance. She specializes in developing structured learning routines and custom educational resources that ensure all students meet their goals.

Based in Olive Branch, Mississippi, Becky also offers private teaching services, bringing the same individualized, student-centered approach she uses in the classroom directly to families who want focused, dedicated support outside of school.

As a mother of a disabled child herself, Becky brings rare personal insight to her work. She understands firsthand the hopes, fears, and daily realities that families of students with disabilities navigate. That perspective makes her not just a skilled educator, but a true partner to the families she serves.

My Approach to Education

Individualized Instruction

I take a growth-first approach grounded in years of IEP experience, setting measurable goals for every student. Whether in the classroom or through private teaching, I design instruction that meets each learner where they are and builds toward where they need to be.

Data-Driven Learning

I continuously collect and analyze assessment data to inform instruction, track student growth, and adjust lesson plans in real time. Progress is never an afterthought. It's built into every lesson.

High Expectations, Personalized Support

I build custom learning paths that challenge students to think deeply and push beyond their comfort zones, while providing the scaffolding and encouragement they need to succeed. Every student is capable of meaningful growth when given the right support.

Inclusive & Student-Centered

I center every lesson around the student's interests, identity, and strengths. When learners see themselves reflected in the content and feel ownership of their education, engagement and achievement follow naturally.

Safe, Supportive Learning Environment

I use proactive behavioral strategies, structured routines, and positive reinforcement to create a space where every student feels safe, respected, and ready to learn. A strong environment is the foundation for meaningful progress.

Community-Centered Support

I serve as a bridge between families and organizations, fostering transparent communication and unified support for the whole child. When parents, teachers, and service providers work together, students thrive.
